## Dataset

Sellers who opted in to share anonymized listing data (via `contribute.py`)
are credited here. Only public listing metadata is shared; all
seller-identifying fields are stripped first (see `DATA_POLICY.md`).

- _Dataset contributors are appended automatically by `contribute.py`._
